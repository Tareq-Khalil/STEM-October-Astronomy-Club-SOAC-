About Us

Who Are We?


ASTRONOMY is a natural science that studies celestial objects and phenomena, exploring the physics, chemistry, and evolution of cosmic bodies and the universe itself. At STEM High School for Boys - 6th of October, we've created this club for astronomy enthusiasts to quench their thirst for cosmic knowledge.

We are a passionate community of stargazers, dreamers, and scientists-in-the-making. Fascinated by the mysteries of the night sky and the majestic views through our telescopes, we gather to explore the infinite cosmos together.

The STEM October Astronomy Club was founded by dedicated students who share an insatiable curiosity about the universe. Our club aims to create awareness in amateur astronomy through night-sky observations, educational talks, and exciting competitions that challenge our understanding of the cosmos.


What We Do?


"Astronomy compels us to look upwards and leads us from this world to another." - Plato

Astronomy transcends theoretical concepts, involving practical observation of celestial wonders beyond our earthly horizon. It questions the origin and existence of our universe, driving our insatiable curiosity and desire to learn.

Our club has successfully built an environment of healthy knowledge exchange about astronomy and astrophysics. Our dedicated team has brought pride to our school by participating in various astronomical competitions held across the nation. We aim for the stars and beyond!

ARMAGEDDON

Date: Annual Event
Venue: STEM High School - 6th of October


Synopsis

Scientists at NASA have discovered an asteroid 65 miles wide, codenamed Destroyer786. This massive celestial body, composed of carbon molecules, rocks, and metals, is on a collision course with Earth. The asteroid travels at 30,000 miles per hour, approaching from the sun's direction with an estimated impact in 1.5 years.

All nations have united, understanding the grave situation and ready to take extreme measures to prevent the end of the world. You are chosen to be part of the core team of scientists tasked with saving humanity.

Your mission: Devise a comprehensive plan to save Earth and humanity. The fate of our species lies in your hands.


Event Structure

  • Two-round competition with online submissions followed by live presentations
  • No economic constraints - all global resources are available
  • 547 days to execute your plan - every hour counts
  • Solutions must be based on current or near-future technology

STAR TREK

Date: Bi-Annual Event
Venue: STEM High School Observatory


Synopsis

This event consists of two exciting rounds: Spacewalk and Messier Marathon. Prepare for an astronomical journey that will test your knowledge and observational skills.


1st Round: Spacewalk

An immersive audio-visual quiz featuring stunning cosmic imagery and videos. Participants must demonstrate their astronomical knowledge through challenging questions.


2nd Round: Messier Marathon

For those who sneak out at night to gaze at the stars and build cosmic imagination - this is your perfect challenge. A stellar hunt requiring participants to embark on a voyage through different celestial bodies using professional telescopes.


What You'll Experience

  • Professional telescope handling tutorial
  • Live observation of planets, star clusters, and nebulae
  • Timed celestial object identification challenges
  • Expert guidance from our astronomy mentors
